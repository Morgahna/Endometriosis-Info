Manage Endo

Manage Endo is a holistic design-led communication and support App to effectively manage endometriosis.

What is Endometriosis?

Endometriosis is clinically is defined by the tissue that lines the uterus growing in places outside of the uterus. However, the reality is often much more piercing. Many women experience life altering symptoms such as chronic pain, infertility, IBS, weight gain, depression, anxiety and much more.

The Facts

  • Endometriosis accounts for $7.5 billion dollars in losses to Australia each year. $2.5 billion relating to direct healthcare costs.
  • An estimated 176 Million women suffer from Endometriosis world wide
